Workshop 2 — Artifact 2

AI/ML Professional Skills Self-Assessment

A structured evaluation of my current abilities across five important artificial intelligence and machine learning competency areas, supported by reflection and evidence from my coursework.

Self-Assessment Prompt Engineering Responsible AI Model Evaluation Professional Growth

Introduction

This artifact presents my AI and Machine Learning Professional Skills Self-Assessment. Together with my AI history timeline, it provides a broader picture of my AI and machine learning knowledge, professional strengths, and areas requiring further development.

Description

The artifact is a systematic self-rating of my current skills in five core competency areas: prompt engineering, AI ethics and responsible use, model evaluation and testing, data preparation, and model development and training.

Each area was evaluated using a five-point scale, with brief explanations based on evidence from classroom learning and practical exercises.

Objective

The objective was to evaluate my AI and machine learning abilities honestly, identify my strongest areas, recognize where more practice is needed, and support each rating with evidence rather than personal perception alone.

Target Audience and Purpose

This artifact is intended for prospective employers, technical team leaders, instructors, and professional partners who want to understand both my technical preparation and my level of professional self-awareness.

The ratings are presented as a professional reflection rather than simply as a classroom checklist.

Process

I completed the assessment using a structured competency rubric. Before assigning each rating, I considered the actual evidence available from lectures, assignments, experiments, and practical course activities.

This approach helped me compare what I believed about my abilities with what I could demonstrate through evidence.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Prompt engineering
  • AI ethics and responsible use
  • Model evaluation and testing
  • Data preparation
  • Model development and training
  • Professional self-reflection
  • Evidence-based self-assessment

Key Strengths and Growth Areas

The assessment identified prompt engineering as my strongest area. It also showed solid preparation in responsible AI and model evaluation.

Data preparation and model development received lower ratings, helping me identify where additional practice and hands-on experience will be most valuable.

Reflection

This artifact challenged me to distinguish between confidence and evidence. Instead of simply stating that I was skilled in an area, I had to consider what examples from my coursework supported that conclusion.

The process strengthened my professional self-awareness and showed me the importance of honest reflection. In future portfolio work, I will continue asking how each artifact demonstrates my skills and what evidence my audience needs in order to trust that demonstration.
